Hi I'm Lucas. I was born in Windsor, Ontario at the Windsor
Regional Hospital. I have always lived in Canada along with
my five other siblings. All but one of us went to St. Gregory
the Great elementary school because it was unfortunatley closed.
That caused me to change schools going into grade 7. I think it
shouldn't have closed as it was a smaller school with close
community members. I am still friends with some of the people
in that community today.

During high school I was always told that I should be a
doctor, and so I took all the science courses I could.
I took physics, biology, and chemestry. Now I see that I
will never have to use them because I had decided that it
was not what I actually wanted to do. Instead I took the
highschool computer courses for three years and decided
that I like creating programs. It feels productive to create
programs that can benefit people in many different ways.

Now I attend the University of Windsor. I think that
I am lucky there is a University in my home town that
I am able to attend. Unfortunatley the Corona Virus has
kept me from attending class on campus which has hindered
the connections I make with others. Hopefully I can get back
on campus to learn new things and meet new people. I am
not sure where I will be in the future, but hopefully it
brings me success and happiness.
